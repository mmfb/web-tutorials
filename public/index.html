Who am I

Miguel Bugalho is an Assistant Professor at the Universidade Europeia since 2010, having also been coordinator of the technology courses. He also taught at Instituto Superior Técnico at the undergraduate level and in the post-graduate program (POSI). He holds a doctorate in computer science and engineering from Instituto Superior Técnico.

He has participated in several research projects, of which the European projects EuTV and VidiVideo stand out. His research is focused in the area of machine learning and data mining in the development and adaptation of tools to different areas such as biology/medicine and education.
